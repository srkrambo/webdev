Week 0: Thinking in Steps

Programming isn't just about writing code; it's about solving problems. The key is to think in small, logical steps.

What is an Algorithm?

An algorithm is just a fancy word for a step-by-step plan. You use them every day, like following a recipe. A computer needs these steps to be broken down into tiny, exact instructions.

Planning an "About Me" Page

Imagine you want to create a simple "About Me" page. You could plan it like this:

  1. Step 1: Add a main heading with my name.
  2. Step 2: Add a paragraph with a short bio.
  3. Step 3: Add a picture of myself.

By turning a big idea into a small list of steps, the problem becomes much easier to solve.
