Week 1: Text & Headings

Text is the heart of most websites. HTML gives you a wide range of tags to structure and give meaning to your text, from giant headings to tiny subscript.

Core Concepts

  • Headings & Paragraphs: Use <h1> through <h6> for titles and subtitles, and <p> for regular paragraphs of text.
  • Emphasis: Use <strong> for important text (usually bold) and <em> for emphasized text (usually italic).
  • Semantic Formatting: Tags like <mark> (highlighted), <del> (deleted), <ins> (inserted), <sub> (subscript), and <sup> (superscript) add specific meaning to your text.
  • Quotations: Use <blockquote> for long, multi-line quotations and <q> for short, inline quotations. The <cite> tag is used to reference the source.
